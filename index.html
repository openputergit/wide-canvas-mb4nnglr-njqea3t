Embark on the Sacred Journey

Experience the divine journey to the four most sacred temples in Uttarakhand.

The Four Divine Destinations

Yamunotri

Source of River Yamuna and home to Goddess Yamuna

Elevation: 3,293 m

Gangotri

Origin of River Ganga and shrine of Goddess Ganga

Elevation: 3,415 m

Kedarnath

Ancient shrine of Lord Shiva amidst snowy peaks

Elevation: 3,583 m

Badrinath

Sacred temple of Lord Vishnu in Garhwal hills

Elevation: 3,133 m

  • September to October

  • First aid kit

  • Rain protection

Important Tips

  • Book accommodation in advance
  • Carry necessary medicines
  • Respect local customs
